# Jelly Zero Math

**Jelly Zero Math** is a colorful neon-style math puzzle game where players combine numbers and symbols to create equations equal to zero.

The game was inspired by a small habit the developer has enjoyed since childhood: looking at the four digits on car license plates and trying to make **0** using mathematical operations.  
That everyday number game became a playful mobile puzzle experience with adorable jelly friends.

## How to Play

Use numbers and mathematical symbols to make the final result equal **0**.

You can combine:

- Addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division
- Brackets `( )`
- Square roots `√`
- Negative signs
- Creative equation patterns

Try different combinations, discover jelly reactions, and beat your best time.

- Cute jelly characters with unique reactions
