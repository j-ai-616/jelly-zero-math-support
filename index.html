Jelly Zero Math

A colorful neon-style math puzzle game where players combine numbers and symbols to create equations equal to zero.

About the Game

Jelly Zero Math was inspired by a small habit the developer has enjoyed since childhood: looking at the four digits on car license plates and trying to make 0 using mathematical operations.

That everyday number game became a playful mobile puzzle experience with adorable jelly friends.

Screenshots

How to Play

The goal is simple: use the given numbers and math buttons to create an equation whose final result is exactly 0.

Try different combinations, check your result, and discover jelly reactions as you solve puzzles.

Button Guide

Numbers

Number Buttons

Tap the given number buttons to add them to your equation. Each puzzle gives you a limited set of numbers, so the challenge is to use them creatively.

Operators

+, −, ×, ÷

These are the basic arithmetic operators. Use them to connect numbers and build an equation that results in zero.

Brackets

( )

Brackets change the calculation order. They are useful when you need part of the equation to be calculated first.

Square Root

√

The square root button applies a square root to a number or expression. For example, √9 becomes 3.

Negative

(-)

The (-) button converts a number into a negative value. For example, using it with 5 can turn it into -5.

This is different from the subtraction operator. It is used to change the sign of a number.

Combine

Combine

The Combine button joins selected digits or numbers into a larger number when the puzzle allows it.

For example, 1 and 2 may be combined into 12, giving you more creative ways to make zero.

Check

Check

Tap Check when you think your equation equals zero. The app will evaluate your equation and show whether your answer is correct.

New Puzzle

New Puzzle

Tap New Puzzle to start a new challenge with a different set of numbers.
